Bacon-Wrapped Pretzels

INGREDIENTS
1/4 c. freshly grated Parmesan
1 1/2 tsp. packed light brown sugar
1 tsp. sweet paprika
1/2 tsp. ground cumin
1/2 tsp. ground coriander
1/4 tsp. granulated garlic
1/4 tsp. freshly ground black pepper Granulated garlic
9 thinly sliced pieces of bacon, quartered crosswise
36 ROLD GOLD® Recipe No.™ 5 Savory Butter Artificially Flavored Pretzel Twists
Ranch dressing and fresh dill sprigs, for serving

DIRECTIONS
Preheat oven to 350º and line a large baking sheet with parchment paper.
In a small bowl, combine the parmesan, brown sugar, paprika, cumin, coriander, garlic, and black pepper. 
Tightly wrap each pretzel with a piece of the bacon arrange evenly spaced, seam-side down on prepared baking sheet. Sprinkle tops generously with Parmesan mixture.
Bake until bacon is cooked through and bites are crispy, 25 to 30 minutes. Let cool.
Serve with ranch for dipping and sprinkle with dill.
