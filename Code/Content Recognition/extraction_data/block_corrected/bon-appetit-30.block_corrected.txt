0.0	0.0	In Sweden the holiday season revolves around one beverage glo gg says chef Marcus Samuelsson Seemingly every single meal during the month of December begins and ends with a glass or three of this warm ruby red mulled wine When I was growing up the moment my grandmother started to cook up a batch on her stove signaled that the festivities were about to begin I knew that delicious spreads of baked hams herring prepared in a variety of ways lutfisk and gubbro ra would be landing on the dining room table in no time Oh and I was amped to realize that it wouldn t be long until I d receive some Christmas presents too Packed with cinnamon cardamom and ginger get ready for your entire home to be infused with the scent of the holidays
