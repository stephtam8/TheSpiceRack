0.0	0.0	There s no better party side than one that can be made ahead You can roast the beets and make the dukkah a coarse Egyptian and Middle Eastern nut and spice blend for this dish days in advance then you ll just need a few minutes to assemble before dinner The dukkah holds well up to a week make extra and use to brighten other roasted veg or meat		
0.0	0.0	This recipe was adapted from The Rise Black Cooks and the Soul of American Food by Marcus Samuelsson with Yewande Komolafe and Tamie Cook

1.0	0.0	Preparation	Preparation	
0.0	0.0	Roasted Beets		
0.0	0.0	Step 1		
1.0	0.0	Preheat oven to 425 Combine beets oil vinegar several pinches of salt and ¼ cup water in a baking dish Cover tightly with foil and bake until beets are tender 50 60 minutes Uncover and let sit until cool enough to handle Peel and cut each beet into 4 wedges or 6 wedges if they are larger	Preheat oven to 425 Combine beets oil vinegar several pinches of salt and ¼ cup water in a baking dish Cover tightly with foil and bake until beets are tender 50 60 minutes Uncover and let sit until cool enough to handle Peel and cut each beet into 4 wedges or 6 wedges if they are larger	
1.0	0.0	Do ahead Beets can be roasted 2 days ahead Let cool cover and chill	Do ahead Beets can be roasted 2 days ahead Let cool cover and chill	
0.0	0.0	Dukkah and Assembly		
0.0	0.0	Step 2		
1.0	0.0	Toast coriander seeds fennel seeds and cumin seeds in a dry small skillet over medium heat tossing occasionally until slightly darkened and fragrant about 3 minutes Transfer to a spice mill mortar and pestle or small food processor let cool Pulse until coarsely chopped Add cashews pistachios benne seeds and ½ tsp Diamond Crystal or ¼ tsp Morton kosher salt pulse again until coarsely chopped	Toast coriander seeds fennel seeds and cumin seeds in a dry small skillet over medium heat tossing occasionally until slightly darkened and fragrant about 3 minutes Transfer to a spice mill mortar and pestle or small food processor let cool Pulse until coarsely chopped Add cashews pistachios benne seeds and ½ tsp Diamond Crystal or ¼ tsp Morton kosher salt pulse again until coarsely chopped
