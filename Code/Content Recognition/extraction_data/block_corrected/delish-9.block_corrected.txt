1.0	0.0	In a medium microwave safe bowl microwave the peanut butter and butter until just melted about 30 seconds Stir until smooth Scrape the peanut butter mixture into a short thin glass Dip 48 pretzels into peanut butter mixture leaving a scant ½ inch at bottom for holding Arrange the PB dipped pretzels on a parchment lined baking sheet and freeze until peanut butter is cold and solid about 20 minutes Reserve any remaining peanut butter mixture at room temperature In another medium microwave safe bowl microwave the dark chocolate chips until just melted about 1 minute 30 seconds Stir until smooth Scrape the melted chocolate into a short thin glass Coat the frozen peanut butter dipped portions of the pretzels in the melted chocolate Return to the baking sheet In a small microwave safe bowl microwave the caramel and heavy cream until just melted about 30 seconds Stir until smooth Using a spoon drizzle over top of coated pretzels If necessary remelt peanut butter mixture so it is a good consistency for drizzling otherwise use right away Using a spoon drizzle the peanut butter mixture over top of coated pretzels Sprinkle coated tops of pretzels with peanuts and mini chocolate chips pressing gently to adhere topping into pretzels if necessary Return baking sheet to freezer and freeze until set about 20 minutes more Enjoy or transfer to refrigerator and cover for
