0.0	0.0	Deselect All		
0.0	0.0	Crust		
1.0	0.0	25 whole chocolate sandwich cookies such as Oreos	25 whole chocolate sandwich cookies such as Oreos	
1.0	0.0	4 tablespoons butter melted	4 tablespoons butter melted	
0.0	0.0	Filling		
1.0	0.0	1 cup creamy peanut butter	1 cup creamy peanut butter	
1.0	0.0	One 8 ounce package cream cheese softened	One 8 ounce package cream cheese softened	
1.0	0.0	1 1 4 cups powdered sugar	1 1 4 cups powdered sugar	
1.0	0.0	One 8 ounce package whipped topping such as Cool Whip thawed	One 8 ounce package whipped topping such as Cool Whip thawed

1.0	0.0	Directions	Directions	
0.0	0.0	WATCH		
0.0	0.0	Watch how to make this recipe		
1.0	0.0	For the crust Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F Crush the cookies until they re fine crumbs Pour the melted butter over the top and stir with a fork to combine Press into a pie pan and bake until set 5 to 7 minutes Remove from the oven and allow to cool completely For the filling Beat the peanut butter with the cream cheese until smooth Add the powdered sugar and beat until smooth Add in the thawed whipped topping and beat until smooth scraping the sides as needed Pour the filling into the crust evening out the top with a knife or spatula Chill for at least an hour before serving	For the crust Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F Crush the cookies until they re fine crumbs Pour the melted butter over the top and stir with a fork to combine Press into a pie pan and bake until set 5 to 7 minutes Remove from the oven and allow to cool completely For the filling Beat the peanut butter with the cream cheese until smooth Add the powdered sugar and beat until smooth Add in the thawed whipped topping and beat until smooth scraping the sides as needed Pour the filling into the crust evening out the top with a knife or spatula Chill for at least an hour before serving
