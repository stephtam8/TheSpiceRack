1.0	0.0	Ingredients	Ingredients	
0.0	0.0	Deselect All		
1.0	0.0	2 cups packed dark brown sugar	2 cups packed dark brown sugar	
1.0	0.0	1 cup 2 sticks salted butter softened	1 cup 2 sticks salted butter softened	
1.0	0.0	2 teaspoons vanilla extract	2 teaspoons vanilla extract	
1.0	0.0	2 eggs	2 eggs	
1.0	0.0	1 1 2 cups all purpose flour	1 1 2 cups all purpose flour	
1.0	0.0	1 teaspoon salt	1 teaspoon salt	
1.0	0.0	1 2 teaspoon baking soda	1 2 teaspoon baking soda

0.0	0.0	WATCH		
0.0	0.0	Watch how to make this recipe		
1.0	0.0	Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F In the bowl of an electric mixer or using a hand mixer beat together the brown sugar and butter until fluffy Beat in the vanilla Add the eggs one at a time scraping the bowl after each one Mix together the flour salt and baking soda in a medium bowl Add it into the creamed mixture in 2 to 3 batches mixing until just combined Mix in the oats until just combined Use your preferred size cookie scoop or a regular spoon to drop portions of dough onto baking sheets spacing them a couple inches apart Bake until dark and chewy 12 to 13 minutes If you d like a crispier cookie just cook a little longer Let the cookies cool slightly on the baking sheets then transfer onto a plate for serving	Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F In the bowl of an electric mixer or using a hand mixer beat together the brown sugar and butter until fluffy Beat in the vanilla Add the eggs one at a time scraping the bowl after each one Mix together the flour salt and baking soda in a medium bowl Add it into the creamed mixture in 2 to 3 batches mixing until just combined Mix in the oats until just combined Use your preferred size cookie scoop or a regular spoon to drop portions of dough onto baking sheets spacing them a couple inches apart Bake until dark and chewy 12 to 13 minutes If you d like a crispier cookie just cook a little longer Let the cookies cool slightly on the baking sheets then transfer onto a plate for serving
