0.0	0.0	How to Store Brownies At Room Temperature Brownies can be stored at room temperature in a low humidity environment To keep them fresh and moist cover with plastic wrap directly over the brownies and store in an airtight container for 4 5 days Freezer brownies can be frozen for up to 3 months To keep brownies moister do not cut brownies until you are ready to eat them
