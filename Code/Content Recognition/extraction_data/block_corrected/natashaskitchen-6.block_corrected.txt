0.0	0.0	Ingredients for Marinated Mushrooms		
0.0	0.0	2 lbs fresh mushrooms quartered halved or even whole if they are small 16 cups water 3 Tbsp vinegar		
0.0	0.0	Marinade Ingredients		
0.0	0.0	1 2 cup white vinegar 2 tsp salt 1 tsp sugar 3 Garlic cloves chopped 2 3 bay leaves 1 2 tsp freshly ground pepper 1 Red bell pepper chopped 1 and 1 2 Tbsp dill weed fresh frozen or dry 2 cups water 1 2 cup vegetable oil		
0.0	0.0	How to Make Mushroom Marinade		
0.0	0.0	1 Make the marinade first before rinsing mushrooms In a medium saucepan combine vinegar salt sugar garlic bay leaves ground black pepper red pepper and dill Simmer marinade for 5 minutes		
0.0	0.0	2 Add 2 cups water bring to a boil and simmer another 5 minutes Lastly add oil and turn off the heat		
0.0	0.0	Prepping the Mushrooms and Assembly		
0.0	0.0	1 Boil 16 cups 4 quarts Water with 3 Tbsp vinegar or juice from 1 lemon to prevent browning of mushrooms		
0.0	0.0	2 Rinse mushrooms immediately after rinsing mushrooms cut mushrooms into quarters halves or even whole if they are small and place in boiling water Stir cover and cook 3 minutes		
0.0	0.0	3 Drain mushrooms and pour marinade over them		
0.0	0.0	4 Boil mixture another 2 3 minutes in the marinade Finally cool to room temp and refrigerate overnight then enjoy		
0.0	0.0	Enjoy
