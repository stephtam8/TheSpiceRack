until it shimmers Season both sides of the tofu with salt and black pepper place in the pan and sear without moving until tofu is browned and golden on both sides turning once halfway through about 8 minutes total Move the tofu to a plate Add 1 tablespoon oil to the pan and add the snap peas Cook stirring occasionally until blistered and just tender about 3 minutes Season with salt and move to a bowl Heat the remaining 1 tablespoon oil add the ginger and garlic and cook until fragrant about 30 seconds Pour in the coconut milk soy sauce and molasses Simmer stirring frequently until the sauce
